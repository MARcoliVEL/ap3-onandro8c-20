Kiritani Ryo (Yoru)

Yoru is a Japanese Duelist agent in Valorant who specializes in infiltration and deception. He uses abilities like a mirror image echo and a blinding flash to create chaos and take down opponents before they know where to look. Yoru's kit is designed for fast-paced, aggressive play, making him a challenging but rewarding agent for experienced players.
